Who are you?

I'm Vik, a user experience-focused full-stack developer.

I just graduated from a front-end development and user experience apprenticeship at a digital product design agency, Fresh Tilled Soil.

If you're curious about all the hands-on experience I gained at my apprenticeship, click here, and I'll tell you all about it.

I get super excited about technology! I write about CSS, javascript and other tools necessary to work as part of a team that creates digital products.

It's my goal to write accessible and thoughtful code, centered around a gratifying user experience.

My recent projects can be found here.

Why do you code?

I code to connect people and technology.

Have you ever been at an airport, desperately trying to load your boarding pass before a flight? Ever attempt to pay a bill online and wound up losing your mind as you struggled to navigate a confusing mass of links?

Maybe you've wasted most of your day signing up for healthcare online or, perhaps, you were charged twice for something because someone didn't take the time to code something correctly.

Design is important - it could be the difference between someone getting the resources they need and someone losing out on their quality of life. I believe code is design - it's just executed on a specific platform or device.

I strive to fit technology in the context of humans, not to force humans in the context of technology.
